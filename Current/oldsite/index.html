Hello, world.

We are Women in Computing (WiC), a developing student group to help provide an academic and social network to connect and support female undergraduates interested in computer science and related fields at Northwestern University.

We are still seeking other founding members to form our executive board - a unique opportunity for incoming freshman and new students!
